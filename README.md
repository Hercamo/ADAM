## 3. What If Your Company Had a Constitution?

**1. Humans Define Intent**
Directors set what the company wants: outcomes, constraints, risk tolerance. They express intent — not commands. They never touch a workflow or approve a routine task.
ADAM has an Exception Economy that strategically governs human involvement at scale: Exceptions & Innovation.

**2. Machines Execute**
A structured mesh of purpose-built agents turns intent into coordinated action. Deterministic orchestration, policy enforcement, recovery built in. Not AI "figuring it out."
Dimensional governance with technical and support ties to 14+ industry and government frameworks.

**3. Evidence Proves Everything**
Every decision produces governance-grade forensic evidence by construction. Audit = playback, not archaeology. Replayable, tamper-resistant, and regulator-ready.
The Flight Recorder and Evidence Objects provide replay with cryptographic locks to prove how, why, and what occurred.

## 5. The CORE Engine

### The DNA of the Company, and What "Good" Looks Like

- **C — Culture**: Values as trade-off priorities. How the company resolves tensions, expresses identity, and behaves under pressure.
- **O — Objectives**: Mandates (non-negotiable), goals (measurable), aspirations. Clear hierarchy agents can't silently override.
- **R — Rules**: Hard constraints: zero tolerance. Soft expectations: exception-tolerant. The guardrails that never sleep.
- **E — Expectations**: Desired behaviors with contextual tolerance. The nuance layer that makes ADAM smart, not just strict.

> Live configuration, versioned like software, testable, rollbackable.

## 9. One Control Plane. 4 Hooks. No Bypass.

*Architectural guardrails that intersect every layer are mandated at every point.*

- **Policy & Risk** — Evaluates every action against legal, ethical, financial, and strategic constraints before execution. The arbiter of "allowed."
- **Evidence & Audit** — Cryptographically anchored proof of what was known, decided, and done. Audit = playback. Flight Recorder enables replay with why and how.
- **Security & Trust** — Identity enforcement, trust boundaries, prompt abuse resistance. Manipulation cannot override execution. Microsoft AGT preferred, integrated.
- **Stability & Drift** — Monitors feedback loops and autonomy budgets. Detects when ADAM drifts from the current DNA graph or enters Skynet territory. The quiet guardian and the ADAM DNA baby-sitter.

> Because these planes are architectural — not procedural — they cannot be bypassed by agents, orchestration, or human pressure.
